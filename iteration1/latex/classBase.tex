\subsection{Detailed Description}
Class representing a immobile base within the \hyperlink{classArena}{Arena}. 

\hyperlink{classBase}{Base} can enhance a \hyperlink{classRobot}{Robot}. If a \hyperlink{classRobot}{Robot} touches the \hyperlink{classBase}{Base}, it becomes a super robot.

\hyperlink{classBase}{Base} have the capability of updating their own position when asked, and also track their own velocity and heading. They have a touch sensor for responding to collision events which is activated/deactivated on collision events.
